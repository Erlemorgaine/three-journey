SHAPES

We can easily make simple shapes, such as:

  • Cube
  • Sphere
  • Cone
  • Torus
  • Plane

Complex shapes (with lots of curves) are a bit more complex,
since we have to do the math ourselves or hope that
someone on the internet did the math for us

Models

...Or, like in CTM, we can load models (preferably GLTF)
into the 3D scene.

3D objects are made up of triangles.
The more triangles, the less performant a model is.

We can optimise the models by reducing the number of triangles, but this will make curved surfaces look less smooth.

So: models with many smooth curves are not so performant

You can do a lot of cool stuff with shadows and lights...

Lights
& shadows

But, again:
it's not performant
if you cast shadows everywhere and use a lot of lights

Interactivity

You can quite easily make the camera of a scene zoom, rotate and pan (on mobile or with two fingers).

Also, doing things on hover and click and following the mouse is fairly easy.

But interacting with an object becomes very heavy for performance when having a lot of overlapping objects in the scene, especially objects with a lot of triangles (like the cloud). The screen will freeze!
