SHAPES

We can easily make simple shapes, such as:

  • Cube
  • Sphere
  • Cone
  • Torus
  • Plane

Complex shapes (with lots of curves) are a bit more complex,
since we have to do the math ourselves or hope that
someone on the internet did the math for us

Models

...Or, like in CTM, we can load models (preferably GLTF)
into the 3D scene.

3D objects are made up of triangles.
The more triangles, the less performant a model is.

We can optimise the models by reducing the number of triangles, but this will make curved surfaces look less smooth.

So: models with many smooth curves are not so performant
